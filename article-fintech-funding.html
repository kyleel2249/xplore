African Fintech Startup Raises $10M Series A

January 2026 • By Xplore Editorial

A Lagos-based fintech startup has successfully raised $10 million in Series A funding, marking a significant milestone in its mission to improve financial inclusion across West Africa.

The funding round was led by a consortium of regional and international investors, signaling strong confidence in the company’s business model, technology, and leadership team.

Expansion Across West Africa

According to the company’s founders, the new capital will be deployed to expand operations into Ghana, Côte d’Ivoire, and Senegal, while also strengthening its engineering and compliance teams.

“This investment allows us to scale responsibly while staying focused on solving real financial challenges for underserved communities,” said the CEO.
